Our Journey

Our Story

CCDM USA Texas Worship Center was founded in 2015 with a simple yet profound vision: to create a place where people could encounter God's transformative love and grow together as a vibrant community of faith.

What started as a small gathering of families has flourished into a thriving community of believers from all walks of life, united by our shared faith and unwavering commitment to serving God and our neighbors.

Today, we continue to build on our founders' vision while embracing innovation to meet the needs of our contemporary community, always staying rooted in the timeless truths of Scripture.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our calling is clear: to lead souls to freedom through transformative deliverance, empowering vibrant faith in God’s kingdom.

Our Mission

To glorify God by delivering souls through fervent prayer, transformative deliverance, authentic worship, and biblical teaching, empowering believers to live free and impact their communities.

Our Vision

To be a vibrant, multi-generational deliverance ministry where God’s mercy transforms lives, fostering faith, freedom, and purpose to shine His love worldwide.

Our Core Values

These values guide everything we do as a church community

Biblical Truth

We believe the Bible is God's inspired Word and the ultimate authority for faith and life, guiding all our teaching and decision-making.

Authentic Worship

We worship God with sincere hearts, expressing our love and gratitude through music, prayer, and lives surrendered to Him.

Community

We believe life is better together, fostering deep relationships where people can belong, grow, and support one another.

Compassionate Service

We serve others with Christ's love, meeting needs in our community and around the world with generosity and compassion.

Discipleship

We are committed to helping every person grow in their relationship with Jesus and become mature disciples who make disciples.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in all we do, honoring God with our best efforts while maintaining humility and grace.
